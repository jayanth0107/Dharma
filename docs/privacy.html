Privacy Policy

Dharma: Wisdom & Astrology · ధర్మ — సనాతనం

Effective: 23 May 2026 · Version 1.0

This Privacy Policy describes how Sanatana Technologies ("we", "our", "Sanatana Technologies") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Dharma: Wisdom & Astrology mobile application (package com.dharmadaily.wisdom) on Android, iOS, or the web ("the App").

We built Dharma to bring authentic Telugu panchangam, Vedic astrology, sacred texts, and devotional content to families worldwide — for free, with no advertisements, and with the minimum data collection needed to deliver those services. This policy explains exactly what we collect and why.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Name and date of birth — only if you choose to use horoscope, matchmaking, family-kundali, or personality features. Stored locally on your device; never transmitted to our servers unless required for a feature you explicitly invoke.
  • Phone number — only if you choose to log in via Phone OTP. Verified via Firebase Phone Authentication. We do not see or store your password; Firebase manages the OTP exchange.
  • Birth time and place — only if you generate a horoscope or kundali. Used to compute astrological charts. Stored locally; not transmitted.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Approximate location (city level) — required to compute panchangam timings (sunrise, sunset, tithi, nakshatram), which are location-specific. We use coarse location only; never precise GPS coordinates beyond what's needed to identify your city. You can change your location manually in Settings instead.
  • Device identifiers — Firebase Installations ID and Android Advertising ID (if available) for analytics and crash attribution. These are not linked to your real-world identity.
  • App interactions — which screens you visit, which features you use, which tiles you tap. Used to understand which content is valuable and where to invest engineering effort.
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data — when the app crashes, we collect the error message, stack trace, device model, OS version, and app version. Used to fix bugs.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your contacts, photos, files, microphone audio, SMS, or call logs.
  • We do not collect precise GPS location continuously in the background.
  • We do not collect health, financial account, or government identifier data.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. How Your Information is Stored

  • On your device — most data (preferences, location, birth profile, bookmarks, premium status) is stored locally in AsyncStorage and never leaves your device.
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud, asia-south1 region) — only anonymous payment records (for premium activation) and aggregated analytics events. No personally identifying information.
  • Encryption — all data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit over HTTPS / TLS 1.2+.

5. UPI Donations & Premium Activation

Donations and premium activation are processed entirely through your installed UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.) using a UPI deep link. We never see, collect, or store your UPI ID, bank account details, or card information. The App only records the donation amount, the plan selected, and a timestamp — keyed against an anonymous device ID for premium-feature unlocking.

6. Children's Privacy

Dharma is not directed at children under 13. While the App contains stories and slokas suitable for children, these are intended to be used by parents and adults with their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at the address below and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

  • Access — view all data stored on-device by opening Settings → Profile.
  • Correction — update your name, location, birth details, language preference, and notification settings at any time inside the App.
  • Deletion — uninstall the App to clear all locally stored data. To delete cloud-stored payment records or analytics events tied to your device, email us at the address below with your device installation ID (Settings → About → Diagnostics).
  • Opt-out of analytics — disable "Allow analytics" in Settings (coming in a future release).
  • Withdraw account — if you've logged in via Phone OTP, you can delete your account from Settings → Profile → Delete Account, which removes your phone number from Firebase Authentication.

8. Data Retention

  • Local data persists until you uninstall the App or clear App data.
  • Anonymous payment records are retained for 7 years for accounting compliance.
  • Anonymous analytics events are retained for 14 months and automatically purged thereafter (Firebase default).
  • Crash logs are retained for 90 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective" date at the top and notify users via an in-App banner. Continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

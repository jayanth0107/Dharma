1. Summary

This Privacy Policy describes how Sanatana Technologies ("we", "our", "us"), based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Dharma: Wisdom & Astrology mobile application — package com.dharmadaily.wisdom — on Android, iOS, or the web ("the App").

Dharma is a free, ad-free, donation-supported app delivering Telugu panchangam, sacred stories (Ramayana, Mahabharata, Bhagavad Gita), Vedic astrology, stotras, and meditation. We collect the minimum data needed to deliver these services. We do not sell your information. We do not share it for advertising. We comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 ("DPDP Act"), the EU General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR"), and the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") for users in those jurisdictions.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Name and date of birth — only if you choose to use horoscope, matchmaking, family kundali, or personality features. Stored locally on your device; not transmitted to our servers unless required by a feature you explicitly invoke.
  • Phone number — only if you choose to log in via Phone OTP (one-time password) authentication. Verified via Firebase Phone Authentication. We do not see or store passwords; Firebase manages the OTP exchange.
  • Birth time and place — only when you generate a horoscope or kundali. Used to compute astrological charts. Stored locally; not transmitted to our servers.
  • Reminders, bookmarks, language preference — stored locally only.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Approximate location (city level) — required to compute panchangam timings (sunrise, sunset, tithi, nakshatram), which are location-specific. We use ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION only; never precise GPS. You can change your location manually in Settings to avoid using GPS altogether.
  • Device identifiers — Firebase Installations ID and (where the user opts in via Android settings) the Advertising ID, used for analytics attribution and crash reporting. These are not linked to your real-world identity.
  • App interactions — which screens you visit, which features you use, which tiles you tap, opt-in donation amounts.
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data — when the app crashes, we collect the error message, stack trace, device model, OS version, and app version.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your contacts, photos, files, microphone audio, SMS, or call logs.
  • We do not collect precise GPS location in the background.
  • We do not collect health data, financial account numbers, government identifiers (Aadhaar, PAN), biometric data, or political/religious-affiliation data beyond your in-app language and content preferences.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites for advertising.

6. Cookies, Local Storage, and Tracking

  • Mobile (Android, iOS): the App uses on-device storage (AsyncStorage) and a small SQLite cache. No cookies are used.
  • Web (dharma.app / play.google.com listing): we use minimal first-party browser storage (localStorage) to remember your language preference and onboarding state. We do not set third-party tracking cookies. Firebase Analytics on web sets cookies named _ga, _gid if and only if you visit the web version.
  • We do not use fingerprinting, cross-app tracking SDKs, or pixel-tracker analytics tools.

7. Log Data and Crash Reports

When the App encounters an error or unexpected condition, anonymous diagnostic data is sent to Firebase Crashlytics. This may include: the error message, stack trace, the screen you were on when the error occurred, your device model, Android / iOS version, and app version. This data does not contain your name, phone, or any content you have entered. It is retained for 90 days and then automatically purged.

8. Storage & Security

  • On your device: most data (preferences, location, birth profile, bookmarks, premium status) is stored locally in AsyncStorage and never leaves your device.
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud, asia-south1 region, Mumbai) — only anonymous payment records and aggregated analytics events. No personally identifying information.
  • Encryption in transit: all data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted over HTTPS / TLS 1.2+.
  • Encryption at rest: data stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore and Cloud Storage is encrypted at rest by Google Cloud using AES-256.
  • Security limitations: while we use industry-standard technical and organisational measures, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a security breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you and the appropriate authorities within the timelines required by applicable law.

9. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information with third parties for their advertising or marketing purposes. We share limited data only:

  • With the third-party processors listed in Section 5, who act on our behalf to deliver specific features (analytics, storage, geocoding, etc.).
  • If required by law, court order, or government authority of competent jurisdiction.
  • In connection with a corporate transaction (merger, acquisition, dissolution) — in which case you will be notified before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • To enforce these terms, protect our rights, or investigate fraud / abuse.

10. UPI Donations & Premium Activation

Donations and premium activation are processed entirely through your installed UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.) using a UPI deep link. We never see, collect, or store your UPI ID, bank account details, or card information. The App only records the donation amount, the plan selected, and a timestamp — keyed against an anonymous device ID for premium-feature unlocking. No personally identifying record is created or transmitted.

11. Purpose Limitation

In compliance with DPDP Act 2023 Section 6 and GDPR Article 5(1)(b), we use your data only for the purposes disclosed at the time of collection. Data collected for analytics is not repurposed for advertising. Data collected for crash reporting is not used for profiling. If we ever wish to use your data for a new purpose not covered here, we will obtain your separate, informed consent before doing so.

12. Children's Privacy

Dharma is not directed at children under 13 (per US COPPA) and is not designed for users under 18 to use without parental supervision (per India DPDP Act). While the App contains kids' stories and slokas suitable for children, these are intended for parent-mediated use.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 in India, or under 13 in the United States, without verifiable parental consent.
  • We do not perform tracking, profiling, or targeted advertising to users we have reason to believe are under 18.
  • If you are a parent or guardian and you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at the address in Section 19 and we will delete it within 7 days.

13. Regional Rights

13.1 India — DPDP Act 2023

If you are a "Data Principal" residing in India, you have the following statutory rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, exercisable by contacting us at the address in Section 19:

  • Right to access the personal data we process about you and a summary of processing activities;
  • Right to correction and erasure of inaccurate or unnecessary data — we will action within 7 days of verification;
  • Right of grievance redressal through our Grievance Officer (see Section 19);
  • Right to nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity;
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time, equally easy as giving consent.

13.2 European Economic Area & United Kingdom — GDPR / UK GDPR

If you are located in the EEA or UK, you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. The lawful basis for our processing is your consent (Article 6(1)(a)) for optional features and our legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)) for essential security and crash reporting. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

13.3 California, USA — CCPA / CPRA

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, correct inaccurate data, and opt out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as defined by CCPA. To exercise your rights, email us at the address in Section 19 with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

14. Your Rights & Choices (All Users)

  • Access — view all data stored on-device by opening Settings → Profile.
  • Correction — update your name, location, birth details, language preference, and notification settings at any time inside the App.
  • Deletion — see Section 15 below.
  • Opt-out of analytics — disable Firebase Analytics collection via Android Settings → Apps → Dharma → Permissions, or by uninstalling.
  • Opt-out of advertising ID — reset / opt out via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID.
  • Withdraw account — if you've logged in via Phone OTP, delete your account via Settings → Profile → Delete Account, which removes your phone number from Firebase Authentication.
  • Disable notifications — Settings → Notifications inside the App, or at the OS level.

15. Data Deletion Request

To delete data we hold about you:

  1. On-device data: uninstall the App, or clear App data via Android Settings → Apps → Dharma → Storage → Clear data.
  2. Cloud-stored data (Firestore payment records, analytics events, phone-auth records) — email jayanthkumar0107@zohomail.in with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include your device Installation ID (Settings → About → Diagnostics). We will action within 7 days of verifying your identity, in compliance with DPDP Act 2023.
  3. Confirmation: we will email confirmation once deletion is complete, with a list of records purged.

16. Data Retention

  • Local data persists until you uninstall the App or clear App data.
  • Anonymous payment records: 7 years (accounting / tax compliance).
  • Anonymous analytics events: 14 months (Firebase default, automatically purged).
  • Crash logs: 90 days.
  • Authentication records: until you delete the account or after 18 months of inactivity, whichever is sooner.

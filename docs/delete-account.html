Delete Your Account & Data

App: Dharma: Wisdom & Astrology · Developer: Sanatana Technologies

This page describes how to request deletion of your account and all data associated with it from the Dharma: Wisdom & Astrology mobile app (package com.dharmadaily.wisdom), in accordance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, GDPR Article 17, and Google Play's data-deletion policy.

How to request deletion

Step 1 — Email us.

Send an email from the email address (or phone number) associated with your account to:

jayanthkumar0107@zohomail.in

Subject line: Account Deletion Request — Dharma App

Step 2 — Include the following in the email body:

  • Your registered phone number (the one you used for Phone OTP login), OR your device Installation ID (find it inside the app: Settings → About → Diagnostics)
  • A clear statement that you wish to delete your account
  • Whether you want full deletion (account + all data) or partial deletion (some data types only — specify which)

Step 3 — We will respond within 7 business days.

After verifying your identity (via OTP to the registered phone number), we delete your data and send you a confirmation email with the list of records purged.

Alternative — Delete locally without emailing us.

The fastest way to remove all locally stored data is to uninstall the app, or open Android Settings → Apps → Dharma → Storage → Clear data. This removes all on-device records: bookmarks, language preferences, location, birth profile, premium status, sankalpa streak. Cloud-stored records (if any) require the email request above.

What is deleted vs. what is retained

Data typeWhat happensRetention after deletion request
On-device data (bookmarks, preferences, birth profile, sankalpa streak, premium status) Deleted immediately when you uninstall or clear app data 0 days
Phone Auth record (your phone number in Firebase Authentication) Deleted within 7 days of verified request 0 days after action
Analytics events tied to your device Installation ID Deleted within 7 days of verified request 0 days after action
Crash logs Deleted within 7 days; otherwise auto-purged after 90 days 0 days after action
Anonymous payment / donation records (amount, plan, timestamp, anonymous device ID) Retained for 7 years for accounting and Indian Income Tax Act compliance, but anonymised — no personally identifying information attached 7 years (anonymised)

What we cannot delete

  • Aggregated, anonymised statistics that cannot be linked back to any individual user (e.g. total daily active users). These contain no personal data.
  • Records required by law — anonymous donation records (₹ amount + plan + timestamp) must be retained for 7 years for tax accounting purposes per Indian Income Tax Act. These contain no personal identifier.
  • Data on backup systems until those backups expire (maximum 30 days after deletion).

Need help?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, contact us at jayanthkumar0107@zohomail.in. We respond within 7 business days.

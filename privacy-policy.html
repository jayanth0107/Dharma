Privacy Policy — Dharma Daily (ధర్మ Daily)

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Introduction

Dharma Daily ("we", "our", "the app") is a Telugu Panchangam and spiritual content application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us.

Information We Collect

Location Data (optional): If you grant location permission, we use it solely to calculate accurate sunrise/sunset times and panchangam data for your location. This data is processed on your device and is NOT sent to any server.

No Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.

No Account Required: The app works without creating any account or signing in.

How We Use Information

  • Calculate daily panchangam based on your selected city
  • Show sunrise/sunset times for your location
  • Send festival and puja reminders (only if you enable notifications)

Data Storage

All preferences (selected city, notification settings) are stored locally on your device. We do not store any data on external servers.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers for serving relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page.
